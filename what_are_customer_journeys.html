What Are Customer Journeys?

Customer journeys refer to the journey or path customers take while visiting your websites or web apps. This usually involves customer activities like adding items to a wishlist, making a purchase, initiating a support query, etc.,

FreshChat presents you with a pre-defined set of such customer journeys using which you can monitor your customers and perform activities like:

  • Sending automated replies
  • Sending order confirmation messages
  • Sending shipping updates and
  • Even trigger a campaign on your website

By clicking icon in the left navbar, you can access the customer journeys page.

  • In the left pane, pre-defined customer journeys are grouped together under various categories like Track Orders, After-sales support, Grow your audience, Recommended for you, etc.,
  • In the right pane, customer journeys belonging to the selected category get listed. You can enable a customer journey by clicking Enable Journey icon. Read how to enable (and configure) a pre-defined customer journey to know more.
  • You also have the 'Create from Scratch' button in the right pane, using which you can configure a customer journey from scratch to suit your requirements. Read creating customer journeys from scratch to know more.
